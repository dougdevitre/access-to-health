## Posting Guidelines

### Frequency
- Facebook: 3-5 posts/week
- Instagram: 3-5 posts/week
- Twitter/X: 5-10 posts/week (including retweets)
- LinkedIn: 1-2 posts/week

### Best Practices
- Include an image or video with every post (3x more engagement)
- Use local images with consent (not stock photos)
- Post at peak times (Tuesday-Thursday, 10 AM-2 PM for most health department audiences)
- Respond to comments within 4 hours during business hours
- Never argue — correct misinformation with facts and empathy
- All crisis-related posts require HCS/HDO approval before posting
