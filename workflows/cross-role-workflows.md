## Workflow 1: Disease Outbreak Response

**Activation**: ≥2 epidemiologically linked cases, OR 1 case of highly infectious disease (measles, meningitis, Ebola)

**Quick sequence**: Report received → EPI verifies → Team assembles (2-4 hrs) → Investigation (4-24 hrs) → Control measures (12-24 hrs) → Monitoring → Resolution → AAR

**Escalation**: Death → state + HDO immediately. Novel pathogen → CDC (770-488-7100). Multi-jurisdictional → state coordination.

---

## Workflow 2: Community Health Needs Assessment

**Activation**: 3-year hospital CHNA cycle (IRS), strategic planning, or grant requirement

**Quick sequence**: Planning + MOUs (Mo 1-2) → Quantitative data (Mo 2-4) → Community engagement (Mo 3-6) → Analysis (Mo 6-8) → Priority setting (Mo 8-9) → CHIP development (Mo 9-10) → Report + release (Mo 10-12)

**Community minimums**: ≥8 focus groups, ≥500 surveys, ≥15 key informant interviews, ≥2 open forums. Compensate participants.

---

## Workflow 3: Maternal Mortality Case Review

**Activation**: Any death within 1 year of pregnancy (direct, late, or pregnancy-associated)

**Quick sequence**: Death notification → Medical record request (1-4 wks) → Case abstraction (4-8 wks) → MMRC review (8-12 wks) → Recommendations → Annual aggregate report

**MMRC composition**: OB, MFM, midwifery, anesthesiology, nursing, social work, behavioral health, community doula, community member with lived experience, EPI, pathology, EMS. Minimum 10 members.

---

## Workflow 4: Substance Use Crisis Response

**Activation**: ≥3 overdoses in 24 hours in same area, OR unusual substance identified

**Quick sequence**: Verify spike (0-4 hrs) → Deploy naloxone/outreach (0-4 hrs) → Public health advisory (2-6 hrs) → Expand MAT access (2-8 hrs) → Community notification (12-24 hrs) → Analysis (1-2 wks) → Policy advocacy (ongoing)

**Always**: Deploy naloxone and fentanyl test strips immediately. Don't wait for data.

---

## Workflow 5: Environmental Justice Investigation

**Activation**: Community reports environmental health concern (odors, contamination, health cluster)

**Quick sequence**: Listening session (Wk 1) → Data assessment (Wk 2-6) → Environmental sampling (Wk 4-10) → Health study design (Wk 6-12) → Community meeting (Wk 12) → Full investigation (Mo 4-12) → Report + advocacy (Mo 12+)

**Key principle**: Start with listening. Community voices first, data second.

---

## Workflow 6: Immigrant/Refugee Health Integration

**Activation**: Notification of incoming refugee/immigrant population

**Quick sequence**: Pre-arrival prep (2-4 wks) → Domestic health screening (Wk 1) → CHW assignment + orientation (Wk 1-2) → Primary care + WIC + immunization (Mo 1-3) → Chronic disease management (Mo 3-6) → Ongoing care coordination (Mo 6-12) → Transition to routine care (Year 1+)

**Week 1 screening**: TB (IGRA), hepatitis B, parasites, lead (children), immunization review, mental health (RHS-15), dental, vision.

---

## Workflow 7: PHAB Accreditation

**Activation**: Health department strategic decision to pursue national accreditation

**Quick sequence**: Readiness assessment (Mo 1-3) → Prerequisites (Mo 3-6) → Documentation collection (Mo 6-12) → Internal review (Mo 12-14) → Application (Mo 14-16) → PHAB review (Mo 16-20) → Site visit (Mo 20-22) → Decision (Mo 22-24) → Maintenance (ongoing)

**Prerequisites**: Current CHNA, CHIP, strategic plan, performance management system, workforce development plan. All <5 years old.

---

## Workflow 8: School Suicide Cluster

**Activation**: Any student death by suicide. CLUSTER ALERT at 2nd death in same school within 6 months.

**Quick sequence**: Verify + activate postvention team (0-2 hrs) → Crisis counselors to school (0-4 hrs) → Staff briefing (4-8 hrs) → Student support (Day 1) → Parent communication (Day 1) → Media management (Day 1-3) → At-risk student screening (Day 1-7) → Extended counseling (Wk 1-4) → Community forum (Wk 2-4) → Ongoing monitoring (Mo 1-6)

**CRITICAL**: NEVER name the method. NEVER create permanent memorials. NEVER hold large assemblies. Follow reportingonsuicide.org guidelines. ALL communications reviewed by postvention-trained professional.
