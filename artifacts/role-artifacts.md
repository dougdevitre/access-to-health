Key sections: Date/location, attendance demographics, themes heard (verbatim quotes), assets identified, concerns prioritized, follow-up commitments

### 10. QI Storyboard (QIC)
See `artifacts/reporting-templates.md` Section 6.2 for full PDSA storyboard template

### 11. Legislative Bill Analysis (POL)
Key sections: Bill number/title, sponsor, summary, health impact analysis (positive/negative by population), fiscal impact, equity analysis, recommendation (support/oppose/amend), talking points

### 12. Grant Logic Model (PMG)
See `templates/grant-and-policy-templates.md` Template 3

### 13. Emergency Preparedness After-Action Report (EPC)
Key sections: Event description, timeline of actions, strengths (what worked), areas for improvement, corrective actions (with owner and deadline), lessons learned, plan revision recommendations
