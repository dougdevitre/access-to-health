# Grant and Policy Templates

> Ready-to-use templates for grant applications, policy briefs, and legislative documents
> Adapt to specific funder requirements and organizational context

---

## Template 1: Letter of Intent (LOI)

```
[Organization Letterhead]

[Date]

[Funder Name]
[Address]

RE: Letter of Intent — [Program/Project Title]

Dear [Contact Name / Review Committee],

[Organization] respectfully submits this letter of intent for [funding opportunity name].

ORGANIZATION: [Name, location, mission (1 sentence), years of operation]

NEED: [2-3 sentences describing the health need with local data. Frame structurally.]

PROPOSED PROJECT: [3-4 sentences describing what you will do, for whom, and expected outcomes]

REQUEST: $[Amount] over [duration]

CONTACT: [Name, Title, Email, Phone]

We welcome the opportunity to submit a full proposal.

Sincerely,
[Executive Director / Health Director]
[Title]
```

## Template 2: Grant Narrative (Federal Format)

```
[PROJECT TITLE]
[Organization Name]
[Funding Opportunity: NOFO # / RFP Title]

ABSTRACT (200-250 words)
[Organization] proposes [project name] to address [health issue] among
[population] in [geography]. [1-2 sentences on need with data]. Using
[evidence-based approach], we will [key activities] to achieve [primary
outcome]. Over [duration], we expect to [measurable result]. Total
request: $[amount].

SECTION 1: STATEMENT OF NEED

1.1 Target Population and Geography
[Demographics, health status, and context of the population served.
Include: total population, racial/ethnic composition, poverty rate,
insurance status, and relevant health indicators. Cite sources.]

1.2 Health Disparities
[Specific disparities affecting the target population. Use equity
framing — structural causes, not individual blame. Disaggregate by
race, ethnicity, income, and geography.]

1.3 Service Gap
[What exists now? What's missing? How does this project fill the gap?]

SECTION 2: PROJECT DESIGN

2.1 Goals and Objectives
Goal 1: [Broad aim]
  Objective 1.1: By [date], [action] resulting in [measurable outcome]
  Objective 1.2: By [date], [action] resulting in [measurable outcome]

Goal 2: [Broad aim]
  Objective 2.1: ...

2.2 Evidence Base
[What research or program models support this approach? Cite studies,
Community Guide recommendations, or model program results.]

2.3 Activities and Timeline
| Quarter | Activity | Responsible | Deliverable |
|---------|----------|-------------|-------------|
| Q1 | [Activity] | [Staff] | [Output] |
| Q2 | [Activity] | [Staff] | [Output] |
| ... | | | |

2.4 Target Population Engagement
[How will the community be involved in design, implementation, and
evaluation? Community advisory board, hiring from the community,
participatory methods. Reference: Nothing about us without us.]

SECTION 3: ORGANIZATIONAL CAPACITY

3.1 Organization Description
[History, mission, programs, service area, annual budget, staff size]

3.2 Key Personnel
[Name, title, qualifications, % effort for each key person]

3.3 Partnerships
[Named partners with specific roles and contributions. Include MOUs
as attachments.]

SECTION 4: EVALUATION PLAN

4.1 Evaluation Design
[Process evaluation: Was the program implemented as planned?
 Outcome evaluation: Did health outcomes improve?]

4.2 Data Collection
| Indicator | Data Source | Frequency | Responsible |
|-----------|------------|-----------|-------------|
| [Indicator] | [Source] | [Quarterly] | [Staff] |

4.3 Use of Findings
[How will evaluation data be used for continuous quality improvement?]

SECTION 5: SUSTAINABILITY

[How will this work continue beyond the grant period? Include:
diversified funding, policy/systems change, institutionalization,
and community ownership strategies.]
```

## Template 3: Logic Model

```
LOGIC MODEL: [Project Name]

INPUTS              ACTIVITIES           OUTPUTS              SHORT-TERM           LONG-TERM
(Resources)         (What we do)         (What we produce)    OUTCOMES (1-2 yr)    OUTCOMES (3-5 yr)
─────────────────── ──────────────────── ──────────────────── ──────────────────── ────────────────────
• Staff (FTE)       • [Activity 1]       • # served           • [Behavior change]  • [Health outcome]
• Funding ($)       • [Activity 2]       • # sessions         • [Knowledge gain]   • [Disparity reduced]
• Partners          • [Activity 3]       • # referrals        • [Access improved]  • [Systems change]
• Data systems      • [Activity 4]       • # materials        • [Policy adopted]   • [Equity advanced]
• Space/equipment   • [Activity 5]       • # partners         • [Capacity built]   •
• Community input   •                    • # trained          •                    •

ASSUMPTIONS:                              EXTERNAL FACTORS:
• [Assumption 1]                          • [Factor that could affect outcomes]
• [Assumption 2]                          • [Policy changes, funding shifts, etc.]
```

## Template 5: Policy Brief

```
[ORGANIZATION LOGO]

POLICY BRIEF
[Title: Clear, action-oriented]
[Date]

THE ISSUE
[2-3 sentences — what is the problem? Local data.]

WHO IS AFFECTED
[1-2 sentences — which populations, how many people, equity implications]

THE EVIDENCE
[2-3 key data points with sources. Include at least one local data point.]
• [Stat 1 — source]
• [Stat 2 — source]
• [Stat 3 — local data]

WHAT WORKS
[Evidence-based solutions. What have other jurisdictions done?]
• [Intervention 1 — result — source]
• [Intervention 2 — result — source]

OUR RECOMMENDATION
[Specific policy action — be precise: bill number, funding amount,
 program design, regulatory change]

COST-BENEFIT
[What does this cost? What does inaction cost? ROI if available.]

CONTACT
[Name] | [Title] | [Organization]
[Phone] | [Email]

[1-2 line organizational description]
```

## Template 6: Resolution Template (Board of Health / City Council)

```
RESOLUTION NO. [####]

A RESOLUTION OF THE [BOARD OF HEALTH / CITY COUNCIL / COUNTY COMMISSION]
OF [JURISDICTION] DECLARING [ISSUE] A PUBLIC HEALTH [PRIORITY / CRISIS /
CONCERN] AND DIRECTING [ACTION]

WHEREAS, [factual statement about the health issue with data]; and

WHEREAS, [disparate impact on specific populations]; and

WHEREAS, [evidence supports intervention]; and

WHEREAS, [organizational authority to act — cite statute or charter]; and

WHEREAS, [community input supports action];

NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the [governing body]:

1. Declares [issue] a public health [priority/crisis] in [jurisdiction];

2. Directs [department/official] to [specific action 1] by [date];

3. Directs [department/official] to [specific action 2] by [date];

4. Allocates $[amount] from [fund] for [purpose];

5. Directs [department] to report progress to [governing body] within
   [timeframe] and quarterly thereafter.

ADOPTED this [day] of [month], [year].

_________________________
[Presiding Officer]

ATTEST:
_________________________
[Clerk/Secretary]
```
