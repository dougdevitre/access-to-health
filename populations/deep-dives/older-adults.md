# Population Deep Dive: Older Adults (OAD)

> Operational guidance for professionals serving this community
> Not a health education sheet — structural analysis, cultural context, and actionable strategies

---

## Health Profile
- **Falls**: #1 cause of injury death in 65+. 1 in 4 older adults falls each year
- **Dementia**: 10% of 65+, 33% of 85+. Caregiver burden is enormous
- **Polypharmacy**: Average 65+ person takes 5+ medications. Drug interactions and adverse effects common
- **Depression**: 7% prevalence, higher in institutionalized. Often undiagnosed ("just part of aging" — it's not)
- **Social isolation**: 25% of 65+ are socially isolated. Isolation is as deadly as smoking 15 cigarettes/day
- **Elder abuse**: 1 in 10 older adults experiences abuse (physical, emotional, financial, neglect)
- **Chronic disease burden**: 80%+ have at least one chronic condition. 68% have two or more

## Screening
- **Falls**: Stay Independent questionnaire, Timed Up and Go
- **Depression**: Geriatric Depression Scale (GDS) — designed for older adults
- **Cognitive function**: Mini-Cog (clock drawing + 3-word recall)
- **Medication review**: Annual comprehensive review by pharmacist
- **SDOH**: Social isolation, food access, transportation, financial strain, safety
- **Advance care planning**: Goals of care conversation — not a one-time event

## Engagement
- **Senior centers**: Hub for services, social connection, health programming
- **Meals on Wheels**: Home-delivered meals + daily check-in + socialization
- **Area Agency on Aging**: Comprehensive aging services network
- **Faith communities**: Churches are central social networks for many older adults
- **Medicare counseling (SHIP)**: Insurance navigation
- **Pharmacies**: Trusted, accessible, frequent touchpoint
