## Population Profiles

### BLK — Black / African American
- **Health profile**: 30% higher CVD mortality, 3-4x maternal mortality, 2.3x infant mortality, 8x HIV incidence, 1.7x diabetes, 40% higher breast cancer mortality
- **Structural drivers**: Residential segregation, employment discrimination, educational inequity, mass incarceration, environmental racism, weathering
- **Historical trust context**: Tuskegee, Henrietta Lacks, forced sterilization, Flint
- **Engagement**: Black churches, barbershops/salons, Divine Nine, Black media, community organizers
- **Key roles**: CHW, PHN, BHC, MCH, EPI, CES
- **Deep dive**: ✅ `populations/deep-dives/black-african-american.md`

### HIS — Hispanic / Latino
- **Health profile**: Higher diabetes prevalence, lower insurance rates, occupational injuries, cervical cancer disparities, TB, Chagas disease in immigrants
- **Structural drivers**: Immigration enforcement fear, language barriers, occupational hazards, educational access, residential instability
- **Engagement**: Promotoras, Catholic churches, tiendas, soccer leagues, Spanish-language media, cultural events
- **Screening**: Use bilingual screening tools (`bilingual/spanish-layer.md`). Clarify public charge — SNAP/WIC/Medicaid do NOT affect immigration
- **Key roles**: CHW, NUT, PHN, HED, CES

### PRG — Pregnant and Postpartum
- **Health profile**: Maternal mortality (especially Black women 3-4x), preterm birth, gestational diabetes, preeclampsia, perinatal depression/anxiety, substance use in pregnancy
- **Structural drivers**: Provider bias, lack of midwifery/doula access, Medicaid gaps (coverage ending too soon postpartum), racism, poverty
- **Engagement**: Prenatal clinics, doulas, WIC, childbirth education, peer support groups, home visiting
- **Screening**: Screen for depression at EVERY prenatal and postpartum visit, IPV screen in private, birth plan preferences
- **Key roles**: MCH, PHN, NUT, BHC, CHW

### HML — People Experiencing Homelessness
- **Health profile**: Life expectancy 12+ years shorter, high rates of mental illness (30-40%), SUD (30-40%), TB, hepatitis, HIV, skin infections, dental disease, foot/wound care
- **Structural drivers**: Housing cost, eviction, domestic violence, mental illness, SUD, incarceration, foster care aging out, medical debt
- **Engagement**: Shelters, street outreach, day centers, syringe programs, libraries, encampment outreach, Health Care for the Homeless programs
- **Screening**: Quick Screen first (low burden). Assess housing, safety, SUD, mental health. Meet where they are — literally
- **Key roles**: CHW, BHC, PHN, CES, SUP

### PWUD — People Who Use Drugs
- **Health profile**: Overdose (leading cause of death ages 18-45), HIV/HCV, endocarditis, skin/soft tissue infections, dental disease, incarceration, stigma
- **Structural drivers**: Criminalization, lack of treatment access, stigma, poverty, ACEs, chronic pain undertreated
- **Engagement**: Harm reduction programs, syringe services, peer recovery specialists, mobile outreach, ED-based intervention. NEVER require abstinence as condition of services
- **Screening**: AUDIT-C, NIDA Quick Screen, SDOH screening. Use trauma-informed, non-judgmental approach. Person-first language always
- **Key roles**: SUP, BHC, CHW, DIS, PHN

### IMM — Immigrant and Refugee
- **Health profile**: Infectious disease screening needs (TB, hep B, parasites), trauma/PTSD (especially refugees), vaccination gaps, chronic disease, dental needs
- **Structural drivers**: Language barriers, immigration status fear, cultural dislocation, credential non-recognition, employment exploitation
- **Engagement**: Resettlement agencies, ethnic community organizations, cultural brokers, faith communities, ESL programs
- **Screening**: Domestic health screening per CDC guidelines. RHS-15 for refugee mental health. Assign culturally matched CHW
- **Key roles**: PHN, CHW, BHC, NUT, HED
- **Workflow**: See `workflows/cross-role-workflows-expanded.md` Workflow 6

### LGBT — LGBTQ+
- **Health profile**: Depression and anxiety (2-3x), suicide attempts (especially youth — 4x), HIV (MSM), substance use, discrimination in healthcare, higher cancer rates in some subgroups
- **Structural drivers**: Discrimination, family rejection, legal barriers, provider ignorance/bias, insurance barriers for transition-related care
- **Engagement**: LGBTQ+ community centers, Pride events, affirming providers, GSAs (schools), online communities, LGBTQ+ media
- **Screening**: Inclusive intake forms (sexual orientation, gender identity). Train staff on affirming language. Use chosen name and pronouns
- **Key roles**: BHC, CHW, HED, PHN, CES

### OAD — Older Adults (65+)
- **Health profile**: Falls (#1 injury cause), dementia, polypharmacy, depression, social isolation, elder abuse/neglect, chronic disease burden
- **Structural drivers**: Fixed income, Medicare gaps, transportation barriers, ageism, caregiver burden, social disconnection
- **Engagement**: Senior centers, faith communities, Meals on Wheels, Area Agency on Aging, Medicare counseling, pharmacies
- **Screening**: Falls risk (Stay Independent questionnaire), depression (GDS), cognitive (Mini-Cog), medication review, SDOH
- **Key roles**: PHN, CHW, BHC, NUT, OHC

### JUS — Justice-Involved / Reentry
- **Health profile**: High rates of mental illness (50%+), SUD (65%+), chronic disease, infectious disease (HIV, HCV, TB, STI), dental disease, traumatic brain injury
- **Structural drivers**: Incarceration as SDOH, employment barriers, housing restrictions, loss of benefits, family disruption, re-incarceration cycle
- **Engagement**: Reentry programs, parole/probation, transitional housing, peer specialists with lived experience, legal aid
- **Screening**: Comprehensive health screening at release. Immediate Medicaid enrollment. 30-day medication supply at release. SUD treatment continuity
- **Key roles**: BHC, SUP, CHW, DIS, PHN

### RUR — Rural Communities
- **Health profile**: Higher rates of obesity, diabetes, opioid use, suicide, motor vehicle deaths. Lower screening rates. Fewer providers per capita
- **Structural drivers**: Provider shortage (HPSAs), hospital closures, distance, limited broadband, economic decline, agricultural hazards
- **Engagement**: Churches, farm bureaus, local businesses, volunteer organizations, schools, Extension Service, telehealth
- **Screening**: Prioritize community-based screening (mobile units, community events). Expand telehealth. Partner with FQHCs
- **Key roles**: PHN, CHW, EHS, BHC, HED

## Intersectionality Guidance

A person is never just one population. Common intersections:
- **Black + Pregnant**: Load both BLK and PRG context. Maternal mortality is the critical intersection
- **Immigrant + LEP**: Load IMM and LEP. Language access is paramount
- **Homeless + PWUD**: Load HML and PWUD. Harm reduction + housing first
- **Rural + Older Adult**: Load RUR and OAD. Transportation and isolation are compounding
- **LGBTQ+ + Youth**: Load LGBT and CHD. Suicide risk is dramatically elevated
- **Justice-Involved + SUD**: Load JUS and PWUD. Reentry with treatment continuity

**Rule**: When populations intersect, the most specific guidance takes priority, but ALL relevant contexts should inform the response. Structural drivers compound — a Black, low-income, rural woman faces more barriers than any single population code captures.
